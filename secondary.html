Our Vision

The Imaginarium envisions a nation where every public education system effectively serves the diverse needs of all children and where every student graduates prepared for success in life

Our Mission

The Imaginarium’s mission is to stimulate and support innovation across Denver to transform learning and public education systems.
The Imaginarium provides multiple distinct pathways for innovation.
  • Classroom and school-level innovation
  • Cohort-based approaches to whole school design and redesign · Individual innovation models

Build community

Inspire and test new models of teaching and learning.

Build the capacity and process to maximize and scale highest-potential ideas.
